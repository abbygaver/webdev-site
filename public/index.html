Our Products

Rocktown Bicycle and Fabrication Company's standard models are carbon fiber mountain and gravel bicycles built to order through a dealer or online and then shipped to the retailer or directly to consumers. They currently have a small model range, but are developing two new bicycles to complement their current lineup. Every bike they manufacture is built with meticulous detail, using high-end components and the best materials.

However, what sets them apart from other small bike manufacturers is that they are a true custom shop. Customers can select their frame size/configuration and RB+F will build a bicycle according to their personal measurements, riding style and specifications. RB+F will build their bike, from hand-layering the carbon fiber frame, to building custom wheel sets and using only the best component groups selected by the customer.
