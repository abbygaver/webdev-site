Rockingham Bicycle + Fabrication was founded in a backyard workshop in 2002 in Stokesville, Virginia near the North River. They are a small business, employing less than 50 people and is privately held, though employees may opt in and invest, making it an employee-owned company. They design and build high-end carbon fiber mountain and gravel bikes in small quantities compared to the big bicycle corporations. Their current corporate headquarters is now in Harrisonburg, Virginia and they just opened a new manufacturing facility in Rockingham County, just north of the city, increasing their production output. Though they are considered a "boutique" bike builder, they intend to expand their product line in the next year. They have established a small, but dedicated dealer network in the Mid-Atlantic and Northeast region states as well as California, Colorado, Washington, and Canada.
