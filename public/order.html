Shop Now - Full-house bespoke options

Our definition of "custom" is a bit different from the norm. While some manufacturer's idea of "custom" is to slap some high-priced shiny farkles on an otherwise "off-the-shelf" frame, we go a little further, depending on your taste [and budget]. Yes, we have some "stock" frames we keep on hand for standard builds, but we also offer a few levels above our showroom floor models:

Stoke Line

Everything you've come to expect from RB+F bicycles: hand-laid carbon fiber frames made entirely in Stokesville, Virginia, with your choice of fork, shock, top-shelf components and wheelsets [all built in-house by our talented staff, by the way]. Everything is assembled, hand-tuned and made ready to shred upon pick up or delivery.

Pro Line*

We fabricate a frame of your choice that is custom-fitted to YOU, by our own experts [i.e., they guys/gals that make your stuff]. You select your model. We take your measurements with our proprietary process, then build your frame. You tell us what you want us to spec your bike with, then we build it and then ship it to you with love and aggression. This is your trail-smashing shred machine, 100% you. Not a model based on "standard" sizes (whatever that is) with a fancy color or different stickers (which we'll do for you, if you want). It's a bespoke bike that's manufactured for your body and will carve up whatever terrain you choose. You even get the details on everything we built, based on your advice, and a build sheet signed by each team member that had a hand in building it, from the sales rep to the mechanic that tunes it before shipping.
